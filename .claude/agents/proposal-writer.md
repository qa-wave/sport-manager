## Priklady ukolu - kdy volat `proposal-writer` v sport-manager

**1. Kdyz** draft proposalu po discovery
   - **Co dela:** pains + scope + criteria + pricing tiers
   - **Co vraci:** proposal + pricing

**2. Kdyz** client chce send me SOW
   - **Co dela:** scope + deliverables + timeline + payment + IP
   - **Co vraci:** SOW draft

**3. Kdyz** revize proposalu
   - **Co dela:** diff + highlight zmeny
   - **Co vraci:** v2 + change log
